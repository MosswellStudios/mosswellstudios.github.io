# Kindred Task — Privacy Policy

[← Mosswell Studios](/)

**Last updated: April 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Kindred Task collects, how it is used, and your choices.

---

## The short version

Kindred Task is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. Your tasks, notes, and voice recordings never leave your device unless you choose to sync them via your own iCloud account.

---

## What data does Kindred Task use?

### Tasks, notes, and projects

Everything you create in Kindred Task — tasks, notes, projects, areas, tags, and subtasks — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data belongs to you.

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synchronised across your Apple devices via Apple's encrypted CloudKit service and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Voice capture

When you use Voice Capture, the app records audio through your device's microphone. The recording is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Foundation Models (Apple Intelligence) to convert speech into a structured task. The audio file is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Microphone access is only requested when you initiate a Voice Capture. You can revoke this permission at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone**.

### Location

If you choose to add a location-based reminder to a task, the app requests access to your device's location to monitor a geographic region. Location data is used solely to trigger that reminder and is never shared with any third party.

Location access is optional. The app is fully functional without it. You can revoke location permission at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services**.

### On-device AI (Kindred Suggest & Voice Capture)

AI features — including Voice Capture parsing and the Kindred Suggest daily planner — run entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework (Apple Intelligence). No task content, notes, or personal data are transmitted to Mosswell Studios or any third-party AI service.

### Notifications

Kindred Task may request permission to send local notifications for task reminders. Notifications are scheduled entirely on-device. No notification content is sent to any server.

### Subscriptions

Kindred Pro subscriptions are processed exclusively through the Apple App Store. Mosswell Studios does not have access to your payment information. Subscription management is governed by [Apple's Terms of Service](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/).

## What we do NOT collect

- Task or note content
- Voice recordings
- Location history
- Advertising identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash diagnostics
- Any data linked to your identity

---

## Children's privacy

Kindred Task is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
