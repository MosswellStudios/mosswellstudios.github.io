# Privacy Policy — Tykket

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

Tykket is designed with your privacy as a priority. Your support tickets, email credentials,
and personal data stay on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Support ticket content or user emails
- IMAP account credentials (stored in Keychain only — see below)
- Usage analytics or behavioural tracking
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data transmitted to Mosswell Studios servers

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — tickets, notes, attachments, and project information — is stored locally on your
device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device except as described
below under iCloud Sync.

---

## IMAP Email Integration (Optional)

If you connect an IMAP email account, your credentials (host, username, and password) are stored
exclusively in the iOS Keychain using Apple's Security framework. They are never stored in the
app's database, never transmitted to Mosswell Studios, and never logged.

Emails fetched via IMAP are converted to local tickets and stored on-device only. Replies are
sent directly from your device to your IMAP server using an encrypted TLS connection.

You can remove your IMAP account at any time in Settings → Email Account → Remove Account.

---

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync in Settings, your tickets and projects are synced via Apple's iCloud
and CloudKit services. This data is encrypted and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
(https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). Tykket has no access to your iCloud data. iCloud Sync
is off by default.

---

## In-App Purchases (Tip Jar)

Optional tips are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your
payment information. Tips are consumable purchases and are not linked to any account.

---

## Notifications

If enabled, reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. No data is sent to
any server in connection with notifications.

---

## Children's Privacy

Tykket is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated
date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
