# Privacy Policy — Reflekt Journal

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

Reflekt Journal is designed with your privacy as a priority. Your journal entries and personal data stay on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Journal entries or their contents
- Mood logs or personal reflections
- Photos or voice recordings added to entries
- Health or fitness data
- Usage analytics or behavioural tracking
- Advertising identifiers

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — including journal entries, moods, photos, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device except as described below under iCloud Sync.

---

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync, your journal data is synced across your devices via Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services. This data is encrypted and governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). Reflekt Journal has no access to your iCloud data.

iCloud Sync is off by default and can be toggled at any time in Settings.

---

## On-Device AI

All AI features in Reflekt Journal — including weekly recaps, writing prompts, journal insights, and the journaling companion — run entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. Your journal content is never sent to any external server or AI service for processing.

---

## HealthKit (Optional)

If you grant permission, Reflekt Journal reads step count, sleep, and mindfulness data from Apple Health to display correlations in the Insights section. This data:
- Is read-only — we never write to Apple Health
- Is never stored permanently by the app beyond the current session
- Is never transmitted off your device

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

---

## Subscriptions

Reflekt Journal offers optional premium subscriptions processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID. Apple's privacy practices apply to all purchase transactions.

---

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, Reflekt Journal schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

---

## Face ID / App Lock

If you enable App Lock, biometric authentication (Face ID or Touch ID) is handled entirely by iOS via the LocalAuthentication framework. Reflekt Journal never has access to your biometric data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Reflekt Journal is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
